Experience a blend of modern luxury and architectural brilliance at Puri Diplomatic Residences, situated in the heart of Gurgaon at Sector 111. Nestled amidst the city's bustling life, these residences offer an oasis of calm and tranquility, where every day feels like a vacation. With a choice of 3BHK+3T+S and 4BHK+3T+S BHK luxury apartments, Diplomatic Residences cater to your unique lifestyle needs, ensuring an enriched living experience.

Spanning across apartment sizes of 2282 to 2800 Sq.Ft, Puri Diplomatic Residences offer a spacious and comfortable living environment. Each apartment is meticulously designed, keeping in mind the need for space and luxury. The rooms are generously spacious, allowing inhabitants to decorate and personalize their space as per their taste and preference.

Strategically positioned along the Dwarka Expressway, Diplomatic Residences provide seamless connectivity to all major points of interest in and around the city. The location advantage ensures you are never too far from the city's essentials, yet distant enough to escape its chaos.

Puri Diplomatic Residences are not just about luxury apartments; they are about creating a lifestyle that redefines opulence. The residences are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including a modern gymnasium, a refreshing swimming pool, a dedicated play area for kids, and beautifully landscaped gardens. All these amenities contribute to providing a holistic living experience that goes beyond the ordinary.
